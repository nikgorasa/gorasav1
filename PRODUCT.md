# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Corporate travel desks and business travelers who need efficient, reliable premium travel booking. The hero serves both as a brand statement for decision-makers evaluating GoRASA and as a fast-access tool for travel coordinators managing bookings. Secondary: premium Indian leisure travelers who value composure over coupon noise.

## Product Purpose

GoRASA is a luxury travel tech platform for the Indian market — flight search, hotel booking, curated holiday packages, and corporate travel management. The hero section's job is to communicate trust and reliability at a glance, then get the user into their workflow (search or tool access) without friction. Not a brochure; a starting block.

## Brand Personality

Confident, Warm, Efficient. Think a heritage Indian hotel's business center rather than a travel agency's flashy window display. Tone is premium but direct — no fuss, no hype, no urgency tricks.

## Anti-references

- Not MakeMyTrip/Cleartrip — no coupon clutter, no discount bomb banners, no rotating deal carousels, no noisy CTAs. GoRASA is quiet and confident.
- Not generic luxury travel — no cliché hero images (infinity pools, sunsets). No thin sans-serif + giant serif headline + gradient accent template.

## Design Principles

1. **Composure, not urgency.** Every element should lower the user's heart rate, not spike it. No countdowns, no "only 2 left", no flashing badges.
2. **One job at a time.** The hero should communicate one clear offer and present one clear next action. Not everything at once.
3. **Trust through restraint.** White space, solid typography, deliberate color. Visual confidence comes from what you leave out, not what you cram in.
4. **Fast entry, not a landing page.** The hero serves a corporate desk user who wants to search or get to their dashboard. Don't make them scroll or hunt.
5. **India-luxury, not western-luxury.** Warm saffron tones, serif elegance, grounded in Indian hospitality aesthetics — not cold European minimalism.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA compliance. The dark hero background must maintain sufficient contrast (4.5:1) for body text. Ensure interactive elements (tiles, CTA buttons) have visible focus states.
